Our Research
Process

How we design, conduct, and publish research that creates real public value.

Research Methodology
01

Research Design

We identify community challenges with meaningful data dimensions. Questions are shaped with input from local partners, community leaders, and the contributor team.

02

Survey & Instrument Design

Structured questionnaires and field instruments are designed by experienced research leads and reviewed for validity and cultural relevance.

03

Field Data Collection

Trained field researchers conduct face-to-face interviews and structured observations across the study area.

04

Data Cleaning & Validation

Raw data is cleaned, validated, and quality-checked by data engineers before analysis begins.

05

Analysis & Visualisation

Data analysts apply statistical methods and build Power BI dashboards and Python visualisations to surface key patterns and findings.

06

Report & Publication

Insight writers and research leads produce the final report. All publications are freely accessible on this platform.
